**Disable full traction on Honda Accord TypeS** - **NOTE!!!* This will disable TSC/VSA on your Honda Accord's, and possibly SRS!!!

The steps to disable traction control starting out and triggering your drive with cutz and buz, do the steps outlined below and the VSA/TSC will be disabled fully, the insturnent cluster backlights (LEDs) will flash randomly and/or distinctively, and the VSA will be turned off completely. 

> (BTW! This was introduced to me long time ago for an Accord07 2oo4 by [Veca B.](https://instagram.com/veca.e30) // [l/inkedin/](https://www.linkedin.com/in/vedran-bajić-530764300/) - Thanks bro 🙏) 

The steps outlined were provided by random person online on some diy, however, it only provides confirmation for the following Honda models: 

Step by step instruction:

- Start car (leave foot on brake pedal)
- Pull up **parking brake** (if not parked already - with handbrake!)
- **Release parking brake**
- VSA button `off`
- VSA button `on`
- Pull up **parking handbrake** again, ie. will be set/trigger to `on`
- Release foot `off` brake pedal
- VSA button `off`
- VSA button `on`
- Press and hold brake pedal
- VSA button `off`
- VSA button `on`
